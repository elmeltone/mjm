Sell Your Home For Free



1.30.2017

It's my pleasure to present FREEALTY.com, an 8-phase project I've been working on for a few months now.

On Freealty, you can list your house both in the MLS and online with no minimum commission to agents.

Freealty makes money by offering escrow, financing, and other services, so the listings can be super cheap. Last year the site was on WordPress and generating some business, but it was never complete and development was ultimately halted with visible bugs remaining.

I took over a total rewrite of the site in November, and while we've only completed 2 of 8 planned phases (and counting), the site is finally developed enough for me to share.

What exactly did I do?

I rewrote the entire code base, moving it from WordPress to a Node.js server. I did NOT do most of the design, but instead concentrated on making everything work independently of Wordpress. Design changes are now ongoing and will be made continuously.

Then, after the first planned design changes, Freealty is adding user authentication, with a dashboard for monitoring listings, escrow, and loan applications. I'm planning to add most of this functionality with React server side rendering.

This is going to require lots of UI design, which is not my strength, so feel free to send web designers my way.

Thanks for taking the time to read my update, and please check out Freealty.com as well!
