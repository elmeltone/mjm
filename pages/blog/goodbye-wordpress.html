Goodbye WordPress!



11.10.2016

I finally kicked WordPress to the curb!

It's been a busy couple of months but I finally found the time to replace my WordPress placeholder site with a blog that I coded myself. Everything here is straight HTML/CSS/JavaScript, coded by hand.

This is great news for me because now I can focus on real, paying projects. Check out my new home page if you have a moment.

Here's what else I'm working on right now:

Enterprise Real Estate Site
I'm rebuilding another WordPress site from scratch in order to make it more scalable. The site owner has big (HUGE) plans and needs a site that he can grow with. This could be a game changer for the real estate industry, and I'm excited to be a part of it.

Cultivation Control Interface
With the coming boom in the cannabis industry, I decided to get involved with a system for monitoring sensor data in grow houses. Basically that means that I'm developing a web and mobile interface for a hardware system built to monitor temperature, pH, and 7 other types of sensor data for hydroponic and aeroponic cultivation. The future is looking very green.

SEO for Yoga
I've been researching yoga retreats a lot lately, but not for vacation purposes. Lots of yoga destinations around the world rely solely on word of mouth and internet marketing, and now I'm working on ranking sites on Google for yoga tourism.

As I finish these projects I'll be sure to share more about them. Thanks for stopping by, and please watch for my next post where I'll finally write about what to do if you're thinking about paying for a coding bootcamp.
